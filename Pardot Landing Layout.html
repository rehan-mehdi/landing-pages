Introduction

In 2022 Greenham Trust will be celebrating its 25th anniversary. During this time we have awarded an amazing £64m in charitable giving to local community projects, both large and small.

  • 1 Please complete the form on the right to select the type, size and quantity of trees/hedging that you require.

  • 2 We will provide up to 25,000 trees to be planted throughout the region. The tree planting season is between October and March so please get your application in ASAP!

  • 3 Please ensure that you have read the full Ts & Cs and gained permission from the landowner before applying.

As part of our 25th anniversary celebrations and supporting the Queen’s Platinum Jubilee, we are delighted to be working alongside West Berkshire Council to offer local schools, councils, charities and community groups the opportunity to plant a tree (or trees) to enhance our local environment for years to come.

Please read the full eligibility criteria and Terms & Conditions on our website: www.greenhamtrust.com/trees
